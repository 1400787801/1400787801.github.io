Team Introduction

The Minnesota Timberwolves were founded in 1989 and are a professional basketball team based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, competing in the Northwest Division of the Western Conference in the NBA.

Despite facing challenges in their history, the Timberwolves have emerged as a formidable force in the Western Conference with the rise of young talents. The team boasts an excellent young core including Anthony Edwards, Karl-Anthony Towns, and other star players.

Known for their resilient spirit and fast-paced offensive style, the Timberwolves are committed to bringing exciting game experiences to their fans.

Team Roster

Anthony Edwards

Shooting Guard | #5

Avg: 27.1 PPG | 5.5 RPG | 4.9 APG

Edwards is the team's leading scorer with incredible athleticism and scoring skills. The 2020 NBA Draft No. 1 Pick, he is regarded as the future superstar of the NBA.

Rudy Gobert

Center | #27

Avg: 13.4 PPG | 11.6 RPG | 2.1 BPG

Gobert is one of the greatest defensive players in NBA history, winning multiple Defensive Player of the Year awards. His rim protection provides a solid interior defense for the Timberwolves.

Jaden McDaniels

Small Forward | #2

Avg: 14.2 PPG | 5.3 RPG | 1.4 SPG

McDaniels is an all-around forward with excellent defensive abilities and improving offensive skills. His height and wingspan make him a defensive weapon.

Mike Conley

Point Guard | #10

Avg: 10.7 PPG | 2.8 RPG | 6.0 APG

Conley is an experienced point guard known for his excellent playmaking and basketball IQ. He brings valuable experience and leadership to the young Timberwolves team.

Karl-Anthony Towns

Power Forward/Center | #32

Avg: 19.1 PPG | 7.6 RPG | 4.9 APG

Towns is known for his tough playing style and unstoppable one-on-one skills, making scoring in the paint look effortless.
